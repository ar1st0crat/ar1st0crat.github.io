My research in brief

Speech data processing based on soft computing models

Key features of the proposed technology: it segments a speech signal automatically on basis of Mel-Frequency Cepstrum (MFCC) and phonological differential features it evaluates weights of speech segments depending on their perception by human (prosodic features are analyzed) it post-processes MFCC coefficients using Principal Component Analysis it processes speech information at several levels: prosodic level, phonological feature level, phoneme level and word level it introduces the FCAS model whose kernel slightly resembles TRACE and LSTM networks
